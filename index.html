About Uncharted

The Uncharted series is a critically acclaimed action-adventure game franchise developed by Naughty Dog and published by Sony Interactive Entertainment. It follows the adventures of treasure hunter Nathan Drake as he uncovers historical mysteries, explores ancient ruins, and battles dangerous enemies across the globe.

Game Series

Uncharted: Drake's Fortune

Released in 2007, the first entry introduces Nathan Drake in a thrilling treasure hunt in El Dorado.

Uncharted 2: Among Thieves

Released in 2009, this game expands the adventure to the Himalayas and is considered a masterpiece in storytelling.

Uncharted 3: Drake's Deception

Released in 2011, Nathan searches for the legendary lost city, Ubar, while confronting his past.

Uncharted 4: A Thief's End

Released in 2016, this final chapter explores Nathan Drake’s retirement, family, and one last adventure.

Main Characters

  • Nathan Drake – The witty treasure hunter and protagonist.
  • Elena Fisher – Journalist and Nathan’s love interest.
  • Victor “Sully” Sullivan – Nathan’s mentor and close friend.
  • Samuel Drake – Nathan’s long-lost brother.
  • Chloe Frazer – Fellow treasure hunter and ally.
